Destination Canada

Destination Canada 2020 is an online event to help answer all your questions and worries, and of course meet new friends!

This event is meant to give incoming students about UBC and aid them with our own survival tips. Of course, you can use this opportunity to meet your potential best friends and test the waters.

Transitioning into university life can be pretty intimidating! We know, from experience.

Event Rundown:
  • 09:30 - 09:45: Check in
  • 09:45 - 10:00: Speech by Linda Bong and Professor Chris Bennett
  • 10:00 - 10:30: Presentation by GISAU and ISOTO (UBC Vancouver and Okanagan)
  • 10:30 - 10:40: Presentation by PERMIKA
  • 10:40 - 10:45: Break
  • 10:45 - 11:05: Question and Answer
  • 11:05 - 11:10: Closing and group picture
  • 11:10 - 12:00: Breakout groups
Note: During breakout groups, students will be grouped based on their faculties (UBCO students will be grouped into one) and parents will have their own group.

Event Details:
  • Time: 9:00 AM WIB; 19:00 PDT
  • Platform: Zoom
  • Audience: Incoming students and parents/guardians
